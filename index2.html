About Us

     We have been manufacturing high-end stainless steel sinks with the most advanced level of expertise in the industry.Our factory and products are certified by international quality standards organizations.

     NANO SINK is a Nano Shield Antibacterials and Antifungal which bring high quality and high technologies Kitchen Sink.

     Our sink is a specially formulated and eco-friendly coating that is applied in the manufacturer for the kitchen sink. Most importantly that it has anti-bacteria feature. It helps you to reduce stain and does not trap grease/oil.

     These perfect sink are made of heavy-duty SUS 304 stainless steel and constructed with thicker gauge steel come with Stone Guard undercoating and rubber dampening pads.
