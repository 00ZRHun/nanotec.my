About Us

Sink Outlet

Sink Outlet, a fusion of German nanotechnology and Malaysian craftsmanship has created avant-garde designs and top-quality kitchen sinks.

Inspired by the life experience of every household, the craftsmen poured their infinite energy and sweat. After 14 complicated procedures, they finally created a durable SUS304 stainless steel sink that is durable, safe, healthy, easy to maintain and textured. In addition to simplifying the housekeeping process, the high-quality sinks can condense the love of a family, let people have more time to share their love time with their families, and a wonderful kitchen experience. Just because we are convinced that each kitchen represents a home.

With such beliefs and principles, our kitchen sink has been exporting to United States and lead the kitchen aesthetics of American families. We bringing the kitchen sink with supreme quality into thousands of families and achieving people’s dream kitchen.
